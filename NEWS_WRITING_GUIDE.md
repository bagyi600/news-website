## Category-Specific Guidelines

### Technology Articles
**Engaging Elements:**
- Start with practical impact: "Your phone is about to..."
- Use analogies: "Think of it like..."
- Include adoption timeline: "You'll see this in..."

**Analysis Focus:**
- Explain the technical breakthrough simply
- Focus on adoption barriers and enablers
- Discuss privacy/ethical implications
- Predict disruption patterns

### Politics Articles
**Engaging Elements:**
- Focus on policy impacts, not personalities
- Use "you" and "your family"
- Include local angle when possible

**Analysis Focus:**
- Explain decision-making process
- Analyze winners and losers
- Predict implementation challenges
- Connect to historical precedents

### Health Articles
**Engaging Elements:**
- Start with relatable scenario
- Include "Talk to your doctor" disclaimer
- Use evidence-based language

**Analysis Focus:**
- Explain study methodology simply
- Discuss limitations and uncertainties
- Compare to previous research
- Predict real-world adoption

### Business Articles
**Engaging Elements:**
- Connect to consumer prices/jobs
- Use real company examples
- Include practical advice

**Analysis Focus:**
- Explain market mechanisms
- Analyze competitive dynamics
- Predict industry shifts
- Discuss regulatory environment

### Science Articles
**Engaging Elements:**
- Start with "so what?" not just "what?"
- Use vivid descriptions
- Include researcher quotes

**Analysis Focus:**
- Explain significance of discovery
- Discuss methodological innovation
- Predict research directions
- Connect to practical applications

## SEO Optimization

### Headlines:
- 50-60 characters including site name
- Include primary keyword
- Use emotional or curiosity triggers
- Example: "AI Breakthrough Could Change Healthcare - NewsHub"

### Meta Descriptions:
- 150-160 characters
- Include key facts and "why it matters"
- Call to action: "Read the full analysis"
- Example: "Scientists discover AI that diagnoses diseases faster than doctors. What this means for your healthcare costs and access. Read the full analysis on NewsHub."

### URL Slugs:
- Use keywords, lowercase, hyphens
- Keep under 5 words when possible
- Example: `/ai-healthcare-diagnosis-breakthrough`

## Performance Tracking

### Engagement Metrics to Monitor:
- **Time on page**: Target >2 minutes for articles, >4 minutes for analysis
- **Scroll depth**: Target >70% for articles, >90% for analysis
- **Comments**: Target >5 quality comments per article
- **Social shares**: Track which insights get shared
- **Return visitors**: From newsletter or saved articles

### Quality Metrics:
- **Fact-check accuracy**: Corrections needed
- **Source diversity**: Multiple perspectives included
- **Update frequency**: For developing stories
- **Reader feedback**: Survey responses

## Workflow Process

### 1. **Topic Selection**
- Choose events with clear "why" questions

### 2. **Research Phase**
- Gather 3+ independent sources
- Check fact-check status
- Research historical context
- Consult expert opinions if needed

### 3. **Writing Phase**
- Start with engaging headline and lead
- Follow appropriate template
- Include mandatory analysis section
- Optimize for SEO and social

### 4. **Review Phase**
- Verify fact-check status
- Check analytical logic
- Ensure reader accessibility
- Optimize for performance metrics

### 5. **Publication Phase**
- Set appropriate category and tags
- Add fact-check badge
- Schedule social media promotion
- Prepare newsletter excerpt

### 6. **Engagement Phase**
- Monitor comments and respond
- Track performance metrics
- Update if new information emerges
- Consider follow-up pieces
